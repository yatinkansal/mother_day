Pyaari Maa,

Agar main aapko zindagi mein ek cheez de sakta, toh woh nazar deta jisse aap khud ko mere aankhon se dekh sakein — tabhi aapko pata chalta ki aap mere liye kitni khaas hain.


Aap hi woh wajah hain ki mujhe achi chezon par yakeen hai. Aapki hansi meri favorite awaaz hai. Aapki awaaz mera safe place hai. Jis tarah aap pyaar karti hain — chupke se, mazbooti se, hamesha — usne mujhe woh sab kuch banaya hai jo main hun.
